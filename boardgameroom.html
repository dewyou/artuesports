BOARD GAME Room

The Board Game Room is connected with the Knight Arcade. Offering a wide variety of board game selections, we even have a table for building LEGOS. The Board Game Arena is the go to place for hosting TTRPGS, or just something simple and fun like Blokus. Students, faculty and staff are all welcome to join!

Game Night

Come join a warm and fun community for board game night at every Thursday 5:00 pm - 9:00 pm hosted in the ArtU board game room! From DnD campaigns to a simple round of board game sessions, or if you want to bring a game you enjoy or your Warhammer 40k figures to paint, we got you covered!
