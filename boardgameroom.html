Board Game Room

The ArtU Board Game Room is a space for students to relax, socialize, and play board games. It is located on the 7th floor of the building and is open to all students.

GAME NIGHT

Come join a warm and fun community for board game night at every Thursday 5:00 pm - 9:00 pm hosted in the ArtU board game room! From DnD campaigns to a simple round of board game sessions, or if you want to bring a game you enjoy or your Warhammer 40k figures to paint, we got you covered!
