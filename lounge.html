GAME LOUNGE

The ArtU Game Lounge (AKA: The Knight Arcade) is an on-campus hub for gamers of all levels; open play, team practices, watch parties, and varsity match days. The arena is located at the heart of the city at 180 New Montgomery St. All the students, staff, and faculty, are welcome. Drop in to play, meet teammates, and learn production skills, where we provide a variety of the most popular games at ArtU.

COMMUNITY

Our community is built on the pillars of teamwork, craft, and a shared passion for play. Whether you're aiming for a varsity spot, want to join a broadcast crew, or just want to meet new friends in the game lounge, there's a place for you here. The conversation, team-finding, and event announcements happen 24/7 on our official Discord server. It's the best way to connect with fellow players and become part of our home for gamers.
