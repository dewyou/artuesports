GAME LOUNGE

The ArtU Game Lounge (AKA: The Knight Arcade) is an on

-campus hub for gamers of all levels; open play, team practices, watch parties, and varsity match days. The arena is located at the heart of the city at 180 New Montgomery St. Students, staff, and faculty, are welcome.
